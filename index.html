Sugar Labs

Whether you're a young learner, teacher, or a budding developer, we believe Sugar Labs has something for you. Read on to learn more about our award-winning Sugar Learning Platform and how to join our diverse community of teachers and learners.

Our mission: Authentic problem solving

The world needs more tools and communities for learn-by-doing education

Learners and teachers already have plenty of tools to help them with instruction-based learning. And despite the known benefits, there are very few tools and communities that encourage project-based learning. Sugar Labs's unique mission is to cultivate a community of teachers and learners engaged in learn by doing and, collaboratively, create tools explicitly made for authentic problem solving.

EXPERIENCE SUGAR

Our educational tools can be experienced in a number of ways. Try the one that's best for you.

Desktop/laptop bootable drive

With Sugar on a Stick (SOAS), you can experience the full Sugar Learning Platform on any computer at any time from a bootable thumb drive.


Desktop/laptop installation

Sugar Learning Platform can be installed as a full operating system on most desktop and laptop computers.


Try now on any device with Sugarizer

Get a taste of Sugar with Sugarizer, which opens in your browser with no installation. Alternatively, you may install Sugarizer onto your mobile or desktop device via Google Play or F-Droid (Android), the App Store (iOS), and the Microsoft Store (Windows).


Try Trisquel for full freedom

Get all the benefits of a bootable/installation drive on the fully free and well maintained Trisquel distro.


Try Sugar on Raspberry Pi

The full Sugar environment on a Raspberry Pi (RPi).


Convenient installation via Flatpak

Do you use a GNU/Linux? Available for any distribution, individual Sugar activities can be conveniently installed via Flatpak.
