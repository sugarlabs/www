LIBRE SOFTWARE CULTURE

Introduction to
Libre Software Culture

One of the primary purposes of Sugar Labs is to introduce and involve people into the libre software culture. Keeping that in mind, Dave Crossland, one of our main contributors, has collected a list of good introductions to libre software culture for those who are new to the culture. Do check out the links below!
